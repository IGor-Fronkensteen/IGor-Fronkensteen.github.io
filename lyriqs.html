🎤 LyrIQs: Aesop Rock Lyric Intelligence

A data-rich exploration of poetic complexity, rhyme density, and linguistic entropy in rap lyrics—starting with Aesop Rock.

🔍 Project Overview

LyrIQs is a natural language processing project that evaluates the poetic and technical depth of rap lyrics. Using tools from linguistics, information theory, and machine learning, I developed a framework to quantify rhyme structures, vocabulary richness, and semantic complexity across artists.

🧠 Methodology

  • Tokenization: SentencePiece and custom syllable splitters for better rhyme capture
  • Lexical Metrics: Shannon entropy, MTLD, Gunning-Fog index, and unique word ratio
  • Rhyme Detection: Regex and sound-based pattern matching for internal and multisyllabic rhyme
  • Visualization: Custom charts showing entropy maps, rhyme webs, and comparative plots across artists

📊 Key Findings

Aesop Rock consistently ranks in the top tier across vocabulary depth, rhyme density, and syntactic entropy. One standout track, “Nightlight,” exhibits rare inversion of sentiment while maintaining structural complexity. Comparative results reveal unique signatures across artists such as MF DOOM, Open Mike Eagle, and Nas.

🎯 What I Learned

  • Balancing interpretability and complexity is key in creative NLP tasks
  • Metric choice shapes the narrative—no single number defines lyricism
  • Art meets data: subjective artistry can be *respected* through objective tools
